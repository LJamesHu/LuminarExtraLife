Information

This year the Luminar cabal has decided to give back to the awesome Secret World community and support Extra Life by summoning over 200+ World Bosses. That is almost 10 World Bosses per hour!

Extra Life is an organization getting gamers to work together and raise funds to help sick and injured children at Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals. To learn more about Extra Life and the Children's Miracle Network, go to http://www.extra-life.org/

Luminar would also like to reward anyone who donates to our Extra Life team with rewards to donors. To ensure you are entered into our raffle or contacted in-game for your loot rights, please make sure to enter your TSW in game name in the "Message to" box on donation. *We will be limiting the amount of World Boss Loot rights to 3 bosses.

Join us in our stream, in our runs, or just say hi.

Luminar would like to thank everyone who has helped us along the way.

Help us support this great cause and help children in need!
